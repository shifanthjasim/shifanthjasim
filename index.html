Welcome to my portfolio! I’m Shifanth Jasim, a Senior Software Engineer and Admin Team member at DCAnalatica, where we specialize in cutting-edge software development, research, and data analytics. I’m passionate about technology and dedicated to delivering innovative solutions that drive success in our ever-evolving digital landscape.

My Journey into Tech

My tech journey began in my hometown of Kandy, Sri Lanka. I was always curious about how things worked, often dismantling household gadgets to see their inner workings. This curiosity led me to programming, and I vividly remember the first time I created a simple HTML page; it felt like magic watching my words come alive on the screen!

In high school, I dove into computer science courses, building a solid foundation in programming. Learning Java introduced me to object-oriented programming, and I found myself spending countless hours experimenting with code and building small projects.

My interest in web development and JavaScript opened a new world for me. I started creating websites for friends and family, which eventually led to my own blog where I shared my tech experiences.

An internship at a local tech company allowed me to work alongside experienced developers, teaching me the value of collaboration and agile methodologies, while honing my problem-solving skills.
